Eaten Mess (Eton Mess)

Description

Eton Mess is a traditional English dessert consisting of a mixture of berries, meringue, and whipped cream. This is a great dessert for a summer evening or a picnic.

Ingredients

  • 400g Strawberries
  • 150g Rasperries
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. Caster Sugar
  • 300ml Whipping or Double Cream
  • 2 tbsp. Natural Greek Yoghurt
  • 4 Meringue Nests

Method

  1. Quarter and hull strawberries, rinse them and the raspberries before placing them in a large bowl. Sprinkle the sugar over the berries and stir until coated. Set the bowl aside.
  2. Whip the cream until you achieve soft peaks. Add Greek yoghurt and stir.
  3. Crush the meringue nests in a bowl.
  4. Fold the cream and berries together, then add the meringue.
