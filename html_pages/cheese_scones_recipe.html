Classic Cheese Scones

Description

The perfect savoury scone - Best eaten warm spread liberally with butter!

Ingredients

  • 225g Plain flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp. Baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • A pinch of paprika
  • 50g Butter
  • 125g Cheddar cheese
  • 140ml Milk

Method

  1. Prehead oven at 200c. Grease baking tray with margarine.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, pepper, and paprika in a large bowl.
  3. Grate cheese and butter on a plate. Rub the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles breadcrumbs Add cheese to mixture, (keep a small amount of cheese for the top of the scones.)
  4. Slowly add the milk until the dough is the right consistency, (Keep a tiny amount of milk for glazing).
  5. Knead the dough into a ball with your hands, avoid handling the dough too much. Sprinkle flour on surface to avoid sticking and pat dough flat until 2cm thick.
  6. Cut out the scones and place onto baking tray. Brush top with milk and add a few strands of cheese to the top of each scone.
  7. Place in the centre of oven for 25 minutes. Keep an eye on them and if the outside is browning too fast, cover with foil and return to oven.
  8. Place on wire cooling rack for 15 minutes.
