Chunky Beef Chilli

Description

Chilli concarne, meaning "Chilli with meat", is a spicy stew containing chilli peppers, meat and often tomatoes and beans. The dish originated in northern mexico or southern Texas.

Ingredients

  • 400g Diced Beef
  • 450ml Beef stock
  • 100g Kidney beans
  • 500g Passata
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 Red pepper
  • 1 tsp. each:-
    • Hot chilli powder
    • Paprika
    • Oregano
    • Ground Cumin
    • Garlic Granules
    • Onion Granules
    • Coriander Leaf
  • Dark chocolate (70-85%)
  • Soured Cream

Method

  1. Prep your ingredients. Measure out the spices in a bowl. Slice and rinse the pepper. Heat the beef stock and sear the beef in a hot pan.
  2. Add diced beef, stock, passata, red pepper and spice mix to the slow cooker and cook on low for 8 hours (or high for 6 hours), stirring occasionally.
  3. Rinse kidney beans and add to pot 1 hour before ready.
  4. Before serving, melt in 2 squares of dark chocolate.
  5. Best served with steamed long grain rice, and a dollop of soured cream.
